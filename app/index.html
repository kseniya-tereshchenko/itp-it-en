Our workflow

01/ Drawing up a roadmap

Our first steps are gathering data on the project, the market and the potential users. ITP managers collaborate with the client’s team on setting the time frame and the milestones as well as optimizing the budget. We agree on a precise development plan, shake hands and sign the papers.

02/ Designing the engine

This is a key stage where we lay the basics of how the system will function and interact with the user. Designers, IT architects and engineers are busy plotting data architecture and user-specific scenarios, creating detailed concepts and prototypes.

03/ System under construction

the project team works hard on translating the plan into a real and functioning product, being sure to follow the best industry practices and guidelines. We carry out progress demonstrations regularly and do our best to make the development process crystal clear for the client.

04/ Flight tests

No product can be ready to launch without precise testing. We make the system go through fire and water by high-load sessions and sophisticated user scenarios, identify and cover up the risk zones.

05/ Ready at runway

That long-awaited moment! We proudly pass control to the client’s team, help and guide them during the launch.

06/ Further service

After the launch we still keep in touch with the client’s team. We maintain the system, handle any issues and welcome feedback. Always there for our partners!

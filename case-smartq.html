How it works

Step 1

Register in the service. Simple, fast and clear.

Step 2

You create a new queue for your clients and tell them the unique keyword.

Step 3

The client sends the unique keyword to the service number and receives the sequence number in this queue.

Step 4

The client receives an SMS notification that their turn has come.

Easy to use

Сreate and manage a queue of your clients. Customers do not need to download the app and register — they can queue and receive notifications via SMS
